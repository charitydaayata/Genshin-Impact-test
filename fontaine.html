Opera Epiclese

Opera Epiclese is a grand, multi-level opera house, complete with raised seating sections and a private VIP area for Furina. The Oratrice Mecanique d'Analyse Cardinale is located on the stage below Neuvillette's seat, which previously generated Indemnitium for the Court of Fontaine through trials.

Romaritime Harbor

Romaritime Harbor is a subarea located in Belleau Region, Fontaine. It is a large coastal elevator and observation deck built on the side of a waterfall vista that extends to an aquabus rail to the Court of Fontaine. It also provides passage to Girdle of the Sands, Sumeru.

Fontaine Research Institute

The Fontaine Research Institute is a research center dedicated to studying machines and energy. Many of its inventions benefit Fontaine, such as the clockwork mechanisms and kinetic cores created by their founder Alain Guillotin. Most mechanical inventions come from the Fontaine Research Institute. Unlike the Sumeru Akademiya, it does not exercise any administrative responsibility over the people of Fontaine or aim to educate.

Elynas

A lush mountain ridge in southern Fontaine that's widely acclaimed for its beauty yet rarely visited by locals and tourists alike. The Melusine village, the Merusea Village, is hidden beneath its mountains and lakes. Legends have it that the tall mountains are what remains of the giant beast Elynas. Erosion from wind and dust has transformed the body of the beast into a home for countless souls.

Chioriya Botique

Chioriya Boutique is a point of interest and an instance located in Court of Fontaine: Quartier Lyonnais, Court of Fontaine, Court of Fontaine Region, Fontaine. It is a fashion label managed by Chiori, a designer from Inazuma.

Sea of the Bygone Eras

Sea of Bygone Eras is an area located in Fontaine. It has its own map tab and exploration progress separated from the main map Teyvat. It is unlocked after completing the World Quest Underwater Nocturne in Series Canticles of Harmony: First Movement - In the Hall of the Sea King.







CHARACTER SHOWCASE

Furina

The "Regina of All Waters, Kindreds, Peoples and Laws" is deeply loved by her people. She follows each and every trial held at the Opera Epiclese with an inextinguishable passion, and is always acutely aware of how the "audience" sees things.

Arlecchino

"The Knave," Fourth of the Fatui Harbingers. A poised, ruthless diplomat. To the children of the House of the Hearth, she is their feared yet dependable "Father."

Neuvillete

In accordance to his role as the Chief Justice, Neuvillette takes the court and trials very seriously, handing out verdicts with impartiality. He is notably perceptive, being able to hear Paimon's whispers and is able to deduce the additional details from the evidence provided to him during a trial. He utilizes the Oratrice Mecanique d'Analyse Cardinale to make the final decision, honoring its request regardless of his own feelings on the matter as per protocol.

Navia

As the head of the Spina di Rosula, Navia is devoted to helping people of Fontaine, being likened as a "big sister" who checks in on everyone. She has a sharp mind and knack for investigating, being able to analyze events from different angles and find answers that other parties may miss.

Wriothesley

Wriothesley is the "Duke" of the Fortress of Meropide, serving as the prison's administrator and overseeing the facility's overall status. Despite his appearance, Wriothesley is fairly calm and collected, not worrying too much about the prison's infamous reputation as he only sees himself as a "leader" of sorts to oversee the population and ensure they have the "tranquility" they desire.
